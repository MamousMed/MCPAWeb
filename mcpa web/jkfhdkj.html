About MCPA

Microsoft Pilote Ariana is a computer science club based at the Pioneer School of Ariana and founded by Ahmed Rekik on April 16, 2015. With around 40 members, our goal is to introduce high school students to Technology, encourage them to discover this area, and develop their computer science skills.
